Jano Games Privacy Policy

Introduction

Jano Games is committed to protecting the privacy of its users. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you use our games.

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal information from our users. Our games are designed to provide a safe and enjoyable experience without the need for user data collection.

Advertisements

Jano Games does not display advertisements in its games. We believe in providing an ad-free environment for our users, especially teenagers.

Target Audience

Our games are primarily targeted towards teenagers. We strive to create a positive and educational gaming experience that respects the privacy and sensitivity of this age group.

Third-Party Services

Jano Games does not integrate third-party services that collect personal information. We maintain a commitment to user privacy and do not share data with external parties.
